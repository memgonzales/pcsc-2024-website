CHI MNL Workshop

Day 1 – May 9, 2024 (8:30 AM – 12:00 PM)

Organized by the Manila chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery Special Interest Group on Computer–Human Interaction (also known as CHI MNL), this workshop aims to catalyze and advance Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) research in the Philippines by fostering collaboration and knowledge exchange among researchers and practitioners.

For more information about the CHI MNL Workshop, kindly visit https://sigchimnl.org/events/pcsc-chi-mnl-workshop
